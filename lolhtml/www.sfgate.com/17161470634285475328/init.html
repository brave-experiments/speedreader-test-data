Roughly a year from now, someone at the Andaz Turks & Caicos Residences at Grace Bay in the Caribbean will step out of their top-floor penthouse and look out over the tropical paradise.

Expected to be completed in early 2021, the 59-key hotel and its 74 luxury residences — including nine penthouses — will open along a 5.5-acre swath of Grace Bay Beach.

The crown jewel, Penthouse H, will anchor a far corner atop the hotel. In addition to four bedrooms and four-and-a-half bathrooms, this penthouse will have a 1,490-square-foot roof deck with a plunge pool. Custom millwork, engineered solid wood doors and floors lined with Spanish porcelain tile will adorn a refined interior prepped for smart home automation.

Floor-to-ceiling windows along multiple walls in the great room will frame views of the ocean, while hurricane doors and windows will provide protection against severe storms.

Upscale finishes like European-style cabinets with soft-closing hinges, sintered stone counters and a suite of Bosch appliances will outfit an open kitchen revolving around a bar island and home to a Liebherr wine cabinet.

Outdoor spaces abound at the 3,768-square-foot penthouse. Besides the aforementioned roof deck, the residence will include a total of six terraces. Each bedroom will have its own private terrace, and the great room and study will open to exterior spaces as well. All told, Penthouse H will have 1,153 square feet of terraces.

More Information

Details

Address: 2 Penn’s Road, The Bight Settlement TKCA 1ZZ, Turks & Caicos Islands.

Features: Four-bedroom, four-and-a-half-bathroom top-floor penthouse will offer 3,768 square feet of living space and a 1,490-square-foot roof terrace with a plunge pool when completed in early 2021. The trapezoidal layout will occupy a far corner of the resort’s highest floor and feature a total of six terraces. Floor-to-ceiling windows will frame views of the water as the refined interior will enjoy soaring ceilings, an open floor plan and an indulgent owner’s suite and spa bathroom with dual vanities, soaking tub and shower.

A resplendent owner’s suite will frame virtually endless views of the ocean and include built-in storage and a dressing area. The suite’s sumptuous spa bathroom will house dual vanities, a walk-in shower, water closet and a freestanding soaking tub.
